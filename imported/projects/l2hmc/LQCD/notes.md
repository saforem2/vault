# Lattice QCD

## 4D $SU(3)$

The wilson action is given by
$$
S_{G} = \beta \sum_{n} \sum_{\mu > \nu}\left\{1 - \frac{1}{3}\mathrm{ReTr}\left[U_{\mu}(n)U_{\nu}(n+\hat{\mu})U^{\dagger}_{\mu}(n+\hat{\nu}) U^{\dagger}_{\nu}(n) \right] \right\}
$$
